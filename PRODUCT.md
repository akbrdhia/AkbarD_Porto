# Product

## Register
brand

## Users
Recruiters and developers looking for a high-tier Mobile & Full-Stack Developer (Akbar Dhia).

## Product Purpose
A dual-version portfolio demonstrating technical depth (IDE mode) and creative mastery (Motion Studio).

## Brand Personality
Expert, Bold, Immersive. (Precise, Kinetic, Maximalist).

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS templates, rounded borders (in 2026), background blurs on nav, gradients on black.

## Design Principles
- Type as the Primary Hero (20vw+ lowercase text).
- The Power of the Void (#000000 pure black backgrounds).
- Purposeful Motion (Narrative-driven reveals via Framer Motion/GSAP).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast (White on Black).
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for all transitions.
- Semantic HTML for keyboard/screen-reader navigation.
