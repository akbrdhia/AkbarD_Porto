# Design

## Visual Theme
Motion Studio / Typographic Maximalism.

## Color Palette
- Background: #000000 (Pure Black)
- Foreground: #FFFFFF (Pure White)
- Accent: #F5C842 (Gold — max once per viewport)

## Typography
- Font: Sora (Sans-serif)
- Headlines: 20vw+, weight 900, lowercase, intentional overflow.
- Measure: 65-75ch max for prose.

## Layout
- 60/40 Asymmetric grids.
- Left-anchored typography (no centered hero text).
- No rounded corners for 2026 components.

## Motion
- Lenis for smooth scroll.
- Framer Motion for exponential entrance reveals.
- GSAP for complex timelines.
- Staggered sequence for list/grid items (<400ms total budget).
